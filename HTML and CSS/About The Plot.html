All You Need To Know About The Plot

So picture this: Aru Shah is just your average teen...until she accidentally wakes up her villain dad, a.k.a. The Sleeper, who’s now after the ultimate cheat code—the nectar of the gods, Amritam—which gives anyone infinite power and immortality. No biggie, right? It turns out that Aru and her four besties—Mini, Brynne, Nikita, and Sheela—are also reincarnations of the five Pandava brothers, epic heroes from Indian mythology.

Mini (soul of Yudhistra, son of the Lord of Death) is the mom friend, wise and cautious. Brynne (soul of Bhima, son of the Wind God) can smash anything and has a zero-tolerance for stupidity attitude. Aru (soul of Arjuna, son of the Lightning God) is a total legend in the making with warrior vibes. Nikita and Sheela (souls of the twin Pandavas, Nakula and Sahadeva) are the ultimate duo and bring their unique chaos to the squad.

As soon as Aru’s powers are “claimed” by her godly dad, Indra (yep, the King of Gods and all things Lightning), she’s thrown headfirst into an adventure. The whole team gets summoned by the Court of the Gods to stop Aru’s dad before he wrecks the world order.

What follows? The squad faces all kinds of mythic beasts, legendary realms, and epic showdowns as they try to save both their world and the divine nectar. Can five (mostly) regular teens defeat a god-level villain and stop him from getting his hands on that OP nectar? Time to find out.
