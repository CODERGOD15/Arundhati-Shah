Who is Krithika Shah?

Krithika Shah is the mother of Aru Shah and Aru's sister (Will be revealed in the spoilers and City of Gold) and was the former love intrest of the sleeper and she owns the Museum of Ancient Art of Indian Culture in Atlanta Georgia. Krithika is a very loving mother and is very considerate to Aru no matter what. I personally love her character alot seeing she's my second favorite side character.
