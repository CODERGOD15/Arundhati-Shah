About Mini Lopez

THE MOM-FRIEND YOU’D WANT

Who is Yamini Kapoor Lopez?

Yamini Kapoor Lopez, better known as Mini Lopez, is the cautious, wise, and loyal friend who’s always looking out for the team. As a reincarnation of Yudhishthira, she’s mature beyond her years and known for her empathy and calming presence. With a natural talent for healing magic, she’s the squad's backbone, the friend who always has a band-aid and a pep talk ready. Despite her fears, Mini faces every challenge with quiet strength and bravery.

Personality Traits

  • Compassionate: Mini has a big heart and always cares deeply for her friends.
  • Practical: Known for her grounded approach and always thinking ahead.
  • Intelligent: Her wisdom shines through in both school and in magical challenges.
  • Dependable: Mini is the friend who’s always there, no matter what.

Powers & Abilities

  • Healing Magic: Mini can heal wounds and injuries, helping her friends in times of crisis.
  • Protective Spells: She’s skilled at casting defensive spells to protect her team.
  • Spectral Sight: Can sense spirits and entities that others might miss.

Embarrassing Facts (Don’t Tell Mini!)

  • Medical Obsession: She once carried a whole mini-first-aid kit into a magical realm... just in case.
  • Afraid of Ghost Stories: Ironically, for someone who can sense spirits, she still gets freaked out by ghost stories.
  • Sticky Note Queen: Has a habit of putting “reminder” sticky notes everywhere—even on friends’ foreheads!
  • Rehearsed Pep Talks: Practices pep talks in the mirror before actually giving them to her friends.
