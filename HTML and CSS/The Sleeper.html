The Sleeper

Before the Darkness

The Sleeper, born as Suyodhana, was once a brilliant mind whose aspirations reached the heavens. Driven by a desire to protect those he loved, he pursued forbidden knowledge to challenge the celestial beings. His tragic flaw? His ambitions surpassed his understanding of power...

The Descent into Shadows

The Sleeper’s journey was tainted by his betrayal of the divine forces. Once a beloved hero, his corrupted path led him down into the depths of darkness. As he grew in strength, so did his desire to surpass even the gods...

Feared by many, revered by few, his actions resonate in the hearts of those who dare defy fate. A tragic anti-villain, the Sleeper was not simply evil; he was a product of a broken system.

Powers Beyond the Mortal Realm

Possessing an otherworldly combination of shadow manipulation and dream manipulation, the Sleeper wields the power to invade minds and distort realities. His mastery over darkness allows him to create illusions and control the fear and desires of his enemies.

His power is not only a weapon but a curse — one that isolates him from the very world he tried to protect.

The Uncertain Legacy

The Sleeper's greatest fear is not death, but the inheritance of his darkness by his daughter, Kara. A prodigy in her own right, Kara's fate is tied to his — a future where she must choose whether to carry the legacy of her father or forge her own path.

The world will never forget the Sleeper, but will it remember him as a hero or a villain? Only time will tell...
