The World of Aru Shah

Hi! I’m Sanvi, and welcome to my website! If you’re a kid who’s into the Aru Shah series by Roshani Chokshi, inspired by Indian mythology, then you’re in the right place! I get that this series isn’t exactly famous, so there aren’t tons of people to chat with about it. That’s why, for my Design project, I created this site—all about Aru Shah—where you’ll find everything you need to know about the books. This site isn’t just for fans; I’m on a mission to make Aru Shah popular—maybe not everywhere, but at least in our school! You’ll find different sections here, and if you’re new, I’d suggest starting with the plot summary. That’s all from me; hope you have fun exploring the site!
